### 🚀 Featured Projects

I am actively developing diverse full-stack projects. Here's a look at my top repositories:

- ❤️ **[LoveMeter](https://github.com/SenthJeya/LoveMeter):** A beautiful, privacy-first Love Calculator built with **React, Vite, and Tailwind CSS**, featuring persistent confetti and a dark-mode UI.
- 🎓 **[University-Event-Management](https://github.com/SenthJeya/University-Event-Managment):** A role-based **JavaScript** dashboard system designed for university events with a multi-level approval workflow.
- 🎬 **[MovieStore](https://github.com/SenthJeya/MovieStore):** A full-stack movie management platform developed with **React** and **Spring Boot**, featuring a dynamic discovery dashboard.
- 🍔 **[Express-Foods](https://github.com/SenthJeya/Express-Foods):** A dynamic food ordering and management platform built with **PHP**, streamlining food orders for admins and users.
- ✅ **[DUTU](https://github.com/SenthJeya/DUTU):** A minimal, clutter-free MERN task management application with JWT authentication.
- 🔬 **[T2D-Epistasis-Detection](https://github.com/SenthJeya/T2D-Epistasis-Detection-Datasets):** Simulated genetic datasets used in my thesis on Machine Learning for epistatic gene detection.
- ✈️ **[Crown-Travels](https://github.com/SenthJeya/Crown-Travels):** React Native travel app for Sri Lankan tourist destinations with Stripe integration.
